Great Pillars of Abesan High School

Welcome to the official website of the 1991 set of Abesan High School Old Students Association. The mission of the AHSOSA 91 is to foster a lifelong relationship between our Alma mater, every member of the association and the general AHSOSA alumni. AHSOSA is made up of old students who make a difference every day. You can find them in industry as entrepreneurs, technologists, medical doctors, social workers, nurses and in a multitude of other occupations. All Abesan High School 1991 set old students living in any part of Nigeria and Diaspora are invited to get involved and become a registered member of our Alumni Association.
